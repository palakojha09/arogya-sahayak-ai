# Arogya Sahayak AI – User Onboarding & Geographical Expansion Plan

## Objective

Expand Arogya Sahayak AI to improve healthcare accessibility for patients and caregivers across different regions of India through multilingual support and community outreach.

## User Onboarding Strategy

* Conduct live demonstrations during health camps and medical awareness programs.
* Collaborate with hospitals, primary health centers, NGOs, and community health volunteers.
* Provide a simple web interface requiring no technical expertise.
* Offer multilingual support so users can interact in their preferred language.
* Create user guides and short demonstration videos for first-time users.
* Collect feedback during onboarding to improve usability.

## Geographical Expansion Strategy

### Phase 1

* Deploy in Telangana through Swecha health camps and partner organizations.
* Support English, Hindi, and Telugu users.

### Phase 2

* Expand to neighboring states by introducing additional Indian languages such as Kannada, Tamil, and Marathi.
* Partner with educational institutions and healthcare organizations to increase adoption.

### Phase 3

* Scale across India through collaborations with NGOs, hospitals, government health initiatives, and telemedicine platforms.
* Introduce offline AI capabilities for rural and low-connectivity regions.

## Community Engagement

* Organize awareness sessions with healthcare volunteers.
* Encourage community feedback through GitLab Issues and user surveys.
* Continuously improve the application based on real-world healthcare needs.

## Future Vision

Arogya Sahayak AI aims to become an accessible multilingual healthcare assistant that helps patients understand prescriptions, hospital bills, and caregiver instructions regardless of their location or language.
