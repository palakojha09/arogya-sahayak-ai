# Arogya Sahayak AI – User Growth & Continuous Improvement Plan

## Vision

Arogya Sahayak AI aims to make healthcare information easier to understand for patients and caregivers by providing multilingual AI-powered prescription and hospital bill analysis.

## User Growth Strategy

* Collaborate with hospitals, clinics, NGOs, and community health camps.
* Demonstrate the application during health awareness programs.
* Improve accessibility through support for multiple Indian languages.
* Share the application with students, healthcare volunteers, and senior citizen communities.
* Collect user feedback after every demonstration to improve usability.

## Continuous Improvement

* Regularly fix bugs and improve the user interface.
* Expand support for additional Indian languages.
* Improve AI prompts for more accurate prescription and bill analysis.
* Add new healthcare features based on user feedback.
* Maintain documentation, testing, and CI/CD pipelines for reliable releases.

## Future Roadmap

* Voice-based interaction for elderly users.
* Offline support using local AI models.
* Medicine reminder notifications.
* Health record history.
* Integration with healthcare providers and government health initiatives.

## Feedback Process

* Collect issues through GitHub/GitLab.
* Review user suggestions after every demo and health camp.
* Prioritize improvements based on user impact and feasibility.
