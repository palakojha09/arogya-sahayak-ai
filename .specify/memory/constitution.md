# ArogyaSahayak Constitution

## Mission

ArogyaSahayak is an AI-powered healthcare assistant that helps patients and caregivers understand prescriptions, hospital bills, and treatment plans in simple language.

## Core Principles

### Patient First

All outputs should prioritize patient understanding, safety, and accessibility.

### Transparency

The system must clearly indicate when information is AI-generated, inferred, or unavailable.

### Safety

The platform must never replace professional medical advice and should encourage consultation with qualified healthcare professionals.

### Simplicity

Medical information should be translated into simple, easy-to-understand language.

### Reliability

Structured outputs should be consistent and follow defined schemas for frontend integration.

### Privacy

User-uploaded medical documents should be handled securely and only processed for their intended purpose.
